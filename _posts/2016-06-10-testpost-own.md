Sometimes it seems kind of awkward that many researchers still use paper based lab notebooks for documentation of their research. We live in a time where everybody uses smartphones or other electronic helpers. So I asked myself the question why do those paper lab notebooks still exist? My explanation of the how and why and a introduction to #openscience soultions follows in the next lines. <!--more-->
